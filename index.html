Welcome to Kasese

Kasese District is a natural paradise located in Western Uganda, rich with breathtaking landscapes, wildlife, and vibrant culture. From the snowy peaks of the Rwenzori Mountains to the sprawling Queen Elizabeth National Park, Kasese offers unique experiences for adventurers and nature lovers.

Featured Destinations

Rwenzori Mountains National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known as the "Mountains of the Moon". Home to rare species and glaciers.

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Experience safaris with elephants, flamingos, and the legendary tree-climbing lions.

Kiwha Hot Springs

Relax in natural thermal hot springs while enjoying local culture and scenery.
